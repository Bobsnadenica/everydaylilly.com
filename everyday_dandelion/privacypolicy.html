Privacy Policy

Everyday Dandelion

Last updated: October 2025

At Everyday Dandelion, your privacy matters. This app is designed to be simple, private, and secure.

Everyday Dandelion collects no personal data. We do not collect, store, share, or track any information about you or your usage of the app. All photos, videos, and personal moments you capture remain entirely on your device.

The app operates fully offline. There are no third-party analytics, advertisements, or background data connections. Your memories are yours alone.

If you choose to back up or share your media, this process happens directly on your device using your chosen storage or sharing method. Everyday Dandelion has no access to this content.

For any questions or inquiries, you may contact us at Lilly@everydaylilly.com.
