Everyday Storage

A simple app for everyday storage — photos of your tire’s expiration date, your shelf storage, or anything you need to remember.

Why Everyday Storage?

Keep track of things that matter. Whether it's receipts, labels, tools, or warranty cards — capture and organize them with ease, right on your phone.

Key Features

  • Photo-based organization: Snap a picture of anything you need to remember — tire pressure, expiration labels, receipts, and more.
  • Quick tags and notes: Add short text or labels to help you search and recall faster.
  • Local privacy: All your photos stay stored safely on your device — nothing uploaded, nothing shared.
  • Simple search: Quickly find what you need through tags or date filters.
  • Cloud backup (coming soon): Optional safe sync for peace of mind.

Use Cases

Everyday Storage is designed for practical memory keeping — the small things you can’t afford to forget.

Vehicle Maintenance: Track tire wear, oil change dates, or car registration renewals.

Home Inventory: Keep photos of what’s on your shelves, in boxes, or storage rooms for easy access.

Receipts & Warranties: Save pictures of documents or serial numbers for fast reference later.
