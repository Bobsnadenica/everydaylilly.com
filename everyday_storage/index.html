Everyday Filetrap

A simple picture database for everything you want to remember — store photos of boxes, documents, or any important item so you never forget what’s inside.

Why Everyday Filetrap?

Because memories fade, but photos don’t. Everyday Filetrap helps you visually organize the things that matter — from ID cards and tools to storage boxes and tire dates — all in one private place on your phone.

Key Features

  • Visual memory storage: Snap a picture of any item or box to instantly catalog it.
  • Tag and note support: Add quick labels or short notes to help you find things faster.
  • Local and private: Everything stays on your device — nothing uploaded or tracked.
  • Smart organization: Filter by tags, date, or categories for easy access.
  • Reminders (coming soon): Get notified when items need checking or replacement.

Use Cases

Everyday Filetrap is your visual memory vault — perfect for organizing the essentials of daily life.

Vehicle Maintenance: Track tire wear, license plates, or service intervals visually.

Home Inventory: Take photos of storage boxes, shelves, or rooms so you always know what’s where.

Receipts & Warranties: Keep photos of receipts, IDs, and warranty documents for easy lookup.
