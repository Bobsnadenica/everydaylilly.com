More Than Just a Photo App

Everyday Lilly transforms simple photos into a dynamic, living journal of your plant's journey.

Precision Capture

How It Works: Bloom in Three Steps

1.

Set Your Reminder

Open the app and set a daily notification for the time you want to capture your lily. We'll give you a gentle nudge.

2.

Snap Your Shot

Use the in-app camera. An overlay of your previous photo ensures perfect alignment, day after day.

3.

Watch It Grow

The app automatically compiles your pictures. Swipe through your gallery or generate a stunning time-lapse video.

Relive Every Day of Growth

Your Personal Gallery is the heart of the app. It's a chronological visual diary where every photo is a milestone. The scroll gallery lets you effortlessly move through the days, offering a unique, tangible feel of the passage of time. Add notes to track watering, sunlight, and fertilizer changes, transforming your photo collection into a powerful plant care record.

  • Photo Notes: Document care routines and observations.
  • Calendar View: Quickly jump to specific dates.
  • High-Quality Exports: Save and share time-lapses easily.

The Bloom Behind the Code

Everyday Lilly wasn't built by a corporation—it was built by a plant enthusiast just like you.

"I started this project when I wanted to capture the magic of my first Peace Lily unfurling its flower. Existing apps were too complex. I wanted a simple, dedicated tool. Everyday Lilly is the result: a passion project focused purely on celebrating growth."

We prioritize simplicity, privacy, and the sheer joy of watching life unfold. Your data stays on your phone, and the app will always remain focused on its core mission: documenting your plant's journey, one beautiful photo at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Everyday Lilly free?

Yes! Everyday Lilly is completely free to use.

Can I use the app for other plants?

Absolutely. While the branding focuses on lilies, you can track the growth of any plant, vegetable, or even long-term personal projects like construction.

Where are my photos stored?

All photos are securely saved on your device (locally), ensuring your memories remain completely private and under your control.

Will there be an iOS version?

Yes, we are actively developing the iOS version. Sign up for our newsletter in the footer to be the first to know when it launches!
